Safety commitments

Least privilege

Shared users receive only the capabilities needed for their role.

Approval integrity

High-risk execution requires fresh approval and cannot be silently replayed.

Continuous hardening

Relay and tool execution paths are iteratively hardened through tests and logs.

Prohibited and high-risk use

  • YonerAI must not be used to facilitate, coordinate, or justify violence, murder, terrorism, weapon construction, child exploitation, human trafficking, stalking, fraud, or evasion of law enforcement.
  • The service is not intended to provide autonomous authority over real-world systems, law-enforcement decisions, or safety-critical operations.
  • Users remain solely responsible for how they interpret and act on outputs. Outputs do not create permission, authorization, or immunity.

Response and enforcement

Containment

We may rate-limit, suspend, revoke, or isolate accounts and traffic when abuse or credible risk is detected.

Evidence preservation

Relevant logs, session records, and audit data may be preserved to investigate incidents, prevent harm, or comply with law.

Cooperation

We may cooperate with hosting providers, platforms, or authorities where reasonably necessary to protect users, defend rights, or respond to legal requests.

Operator status and limits

YonerAI is currently operated by a minor individual developer. That means the service is developed and moderated with best effort, but it should not be treated as a guaranteed enterprise-grade safety or legal-response program.

If users misuse the service for unlawful or violent conduct, responsibility remains with those users. YonerAI and its developer do not endorse, direct, or participate in such conduct.
